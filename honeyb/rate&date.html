Activities

Part of the activities our users can hope to complete while they use our service are to purchase from our high quality honey and leave a comment about their experience with our product.

Comments and Payment process

A visitor who desires to take part in our exceptional product, would need to purchase the product from our large list of collections. At first the visitor needs to choose an amount of the product they wish to purchase, then they will fill a list of information. It requires their first and last name, then email address and also their phone number. Followed by the card number they will use, alongside the three digits code in that back of the card and the zip code associated with the card. This will be for identification and validation purposes. They will need to confirm the transaction by allowing us to complete it on our side. In case someone wants to leave a comment on our website based on their past experiences with us. They will need to access the review area, then select and accept our policy for a safe and respected platform. Afterwards they will proceed in the comment area and freely type about their experience with us.
